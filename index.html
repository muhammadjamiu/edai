Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about the cohort, schedule, and how we may split tracks if there’s enough interest.

Who is this for?

Middle (Grade 5+) and High School students. We explicitly welcome late elementary students (5th grade) who are ready to build.

Will there be separate batches for Middle and High School?

Possibly. If we receive enough interest from both groups, we’ll create separate batches for Middle School and High School.

What are the weekly hours?

-Saturdays 9 AM – 12:30 PM

-Total of 28 hours across 8 weeks

-Available in California and Washington State.

When does the program start?

We are accepting applications for 2026 on a rolling basis. Apply now and we will follow up with specific start dates, in shaa Allah.

How much does it cost?

$800 total for the full 8-week accelerator. Students must bring their own laptop.
